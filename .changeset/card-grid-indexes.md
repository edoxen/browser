Card-grid views for the meetings and resolutions indexes.

- New shared `.edoxen-card-grid` layout (responsive `auto-fill` grid, ~300px cards) applied to the meetings index, the decisions/resolutions index, the search-filter island's results, and the home page's recent-cards sections.
- Island result cards switch from full-width rows to grid cards; the meeting chip is pinned to the card bottom.
- `DecisionList`/`MeetingList` keep the dense column layout for their `compact` variants (e.g. decisions adopted at a meeting on the meeting detail page).
